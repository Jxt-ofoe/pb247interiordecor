About P.B 247 Interior Decor

Crafted
with care

Designed around
the way you live.

P.B 247 Interior Decor is an interior furniture and metal works shop. Our projects include educational centers, healthcare facilities, government offices and select retail locations. We serve the Greater Accra metropolitan area including the Tema, Kasoa, Agbogba, Dodowa and the broader Ghana market. We also do delivery service at a cost so just place your order and it can be delivered to you.

We serve the Greater Accra metropolitan area including Tema, Kasoa, Agbogba, Dodowa and the broader Ghana market.

Yes. We provide delivery service at a cost, so once your order is placed we can arrange delivery to your preferred destination.
